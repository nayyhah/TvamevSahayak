Welcome to Tvamev Sahayak

त्वमेव is the hindi word for you and सहायक is Hindi for assistant/ helper. Together when combined Tvamev Sahayak means You are a helper.

Tvamev Sahayak is built on the principle that everyone is a helper , for themlseves and fbefore and above that , themselves. We encourage the use of empathy and not sympathy , the latter holds a negative conotation and a sense of pity while the former is being able to feel others's pain. It envisions to grow as an idea across regions , economies and countries using this principle that everyone is born a helper and it is our duty to help other in becoming their own and others' helpers.

What we do

त्वमेव सहायक

Advocate Mental Health

We aim to destigmatize mental health by raising awareness about it.

Reduce Illiteracy

We aim to reduce illiteracy by educating about crucial social issues.

Educate People

We aim to educate people about prevalent issues like dowry, rape and abuse.

Advertise Sustainable Lifestyle

We aim to promote healthy lifestyle choices amongst the general public.

Support Women's Rights

We aim to support and raise awareness about women's rights and rights of people with disabilities.

Support LGBTQ+ community

We aim to support and be an ally of the LGBTQ+ community by raising awareness about them.
