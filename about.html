Welcome to Tvamev Sahayak

त्वमेव is the hindi word for you and सहायक is Hindi for assistant/ helper. Together when combined Tvamev Sahayak means You are a helper.

Tvamev Sahayak is built on the principle that everyone is a helper , for themlseves and before and above that , themselves. We encourage the use of empathy and not sympathy , the latter holds a negative conotation and a sense of pity while the former is being able to feel others's pain. It envisions to grow as an idea across regions , economies and countries using this principle that everyone is born a helper and it is our duty to help others in becoming their own and others' helpers.
